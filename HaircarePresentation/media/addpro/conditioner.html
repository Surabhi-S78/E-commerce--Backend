Repair damaged hair and get softer locks with L'Oréal Paris Total Repair 5 Restoring Conditioner

  • 192.5 ML
  • 71.5 ML
Product Details

L'Oréal Paris Total Repair 5 Restoring Conditioner magnificently tackles the five visible signs of damaged hair - Hair fall, Dryness, Roughness, Dullness and Split ends. Damaged hair usually lacks the natural cement that is necessary to make the hair strong and resilient. In order to ensure cohesion and strength, the L'Oréal team has integrated the Keratin XS technology to compensate the hair with the natural cement. Keratin XS technology reaches the inner layer of the fibres and ensures faster repair.
Highlights:

1. Boosted with up to 17% Keratin XS + Repair concentrate
2. Helps fight against the five visible signs of damaged hair - hair fall, dryness, roughness, dullness, and split ends
3. Ensures 5X stronger and renewed hair from the first use
